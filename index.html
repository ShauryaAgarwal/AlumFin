About AlumFin

AlumFin is a platform that provides alumni-supported education funding and mentorship for college and higher-education students. We offer microfinancing options where students can connect with alumni, receive one-on-one verification, and secure student loans up to $5000 at lower interest rates. Additionally, students can connect with peers from similar backgrounds to gain valuable advice and guidance.

Our Mission: To empower students by connecting them with alumni for financial aid and mentorship.

Problem Statement

There exists a lack of accessible and comprehensive support for students in higher education, including financial resources, career prospects, and mentorship opportunities. Furthermore, not having a clear communication channel with alumni inhibits prospective students from learning about the different support structures available to them.

Headwinds

There are regulatory hurdles to set up a micro-financing company. It may also be challenging to compete with traditional banks and build credibility and trust. User adoption may be a hurdle too because it would require alumni engagement without significant incentive.

Tailwinds

Our startup addresses a pressing social issue by connecting alumni with students in need. This alignment with social impact resonates with individuals and organizations that prioritize philanthropy and education, creating support and interest.

Competitors

There exist both direct and indirect competitors within the micro-financing space for students, including SoFi (prominent in the lending market) and Earnest (specializing in loan refinancing). Traditional financial institutions exist as indirect competitors, as they differ in the aspect of personalized financing. Our product's emphasis on alumni mentorship allows for a unique method of micro-financing within the existing market.
